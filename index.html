Rules of Connect 4

  • 1. Connect 4 is a two-player strategy game played on a vertical grid of 7 columns and 6 rows.
  • 2. The objective of the game is to be the first to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of four of one's own discs.
  • 3. Players take turns dropping colored discs from the top into a column. The disc falls down to the lowest unoccupied row in that column.
  • 4. The game ends when one player achieves a line of four discs, or when the board is full and no more moves can be made.
  • 5. If the board is full and no player has achieved a line of four discs, the game is a draw.
